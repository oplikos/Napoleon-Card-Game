Welcome to Napoleon

A classic solitaire-style card game with a twist. Arrange the cards by suit and order with clever moves. Here's how it works:

  • The game board has 3 columns and 5 rows.
  • Cards are dealt row-by-row: first to the left column, then the right.
  • If an Ace appears and the middle column of that row is empty, the Ace goes to the middle instead. A new card replaces it in the original spot.
  • Only the top card in any stack can be moved.
  • You can only move a card onto another card of the same suit and in sequence (+1 or -1).
  • Empty rows can accept any card.
  • Cards in the middle (Ace stack) are placed directly on top of each other with no offset.
